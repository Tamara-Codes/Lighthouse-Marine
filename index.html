Temporary Employment

We provide services as temporary employment agency for the maritime industry for seafarers and onshore personnel. Our employees are experienced in the European and Global environment and are used to work for longer assignments outside of their home countries. Here, too, we adapt to the personnel requirements of each project and accompany the employees during the initial preparation.

Crew Management and Recruitment

We provide comprehensive crew vessel management solutions to the marine industry. With our close cooperation with our offices and partner companies in Germany, Rijeka, Jakarta, Manila and Monaco enables us to provide clients international seafarers for different types of vessels.

Foreign Employment

We can hire and employ your crew directly for the benefits of different European or global nations. We have experts in different countries with the best qualifications to meet MLC 2006, local or flag state requirements.

About us

We offer excellent service for companies in the maritime industry. We specialize in sourcing, placement and direct employment of sea personnel as well as having own payroll system and financial accounting with international and specific expertise. We offer industry-leading service. Our goal is perfect results and the satisfaction of our customers always has the highest priority.

We introduce ourselves

Our secret? We love our work. With over 30 years of experience in the maritime industry, we are a highly reliable partner. We accept every challenge and dedicate ourselves to our projects with maximum energy and attention.

Strategy and planning

New expertise

With our new Managing Director we have access to a deep knowledge about the Crew Management and Operations for almost all ship types, have wider access to seafaring personnel from various locations around the world.

Certified by official authorities
